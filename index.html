Saetbyeol Leeyouk

Designer, creative coder, and AI engineer who uses data and computational methods to work toward gender justice. Her work is based on the intersectional approach containing feminism, human-animal and human-tech-society relationships. She uses XR, AI, and interactive web development skills as a method for making people join the journey for a better society.
